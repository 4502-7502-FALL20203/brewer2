Best restaurants in Columbia, MO

Columbia has a wide range of diverse restaurants downtown and across the city. Down below are seven popular spots try if you’re just visiting or if you live nearby.

Downtown

Logboat Brewing Company

504 Fay St, Columbia, MO 65201

One of the many breweries in Columbia, Logboat supplies beer to bars and restaurants all across town. Among the many brews Logboat offers, one of their most popular drinks is the Snapper IPA.

Website >> (573) 397-6786

Ozark Mountain Biscuit Co.

1204 Hinkson Ave, Columbia, MO 65201

Ozark Mountain’s food truck can always be seen somewhere on Mizzou’s campus, downtown or at the farmers market. Along with having a food truck, Ozark Mountain has a permanent location on Hinkson Avenue with a an extensive menu. Favorites are the fried chicken dinner, fried catfish, and Papaw’s Pancakes.

Website>> (573) 447-6547

Café Berlin

220 N 10th St, Columbia, MO 65201 Café Berlin has one of the best brunch menus in all of Columbia. This restaurant serves breakfast burritos, pancakes, tacos, along with so much more.

Website >> (573) 555-2424

Ernie'e Café & Steakhouse

Ernie’s Café is one of the most popular breakfast spots in Columbia. There are even lines out the door of people wanting to get a table. It has a vintage interior, and their menu includes hashbrowns, sausage, eggs, hotcakes and more.

Website>> (573) 441-0400

Teller’s Restaurant

820 E Broadway, Columbia, MO 65201

Teller’s is located in downtown Columbia and is a unique spot with tons of local art covering the walls. Their diverse menu includes sandwiches, wraps, entrees, pastas and more. They also have a great cocktail menu, a local favorite are their martinis.

Website>> (573) 441-8355

Geisha Sushi Bar

804 E Broadway, Columbia, MO 65201

Columbia has a wide variety of sushi spots, but Geisha is one of the most popular and is worth a visit. The offer fun boat-shaped platters with rolls and nigiri on top with dry ice smoke coming from the bottom of the boat. If you’re looking for a restaurant with good variety and quality rolls, Geisha is the place to go.

Website>> (573) 777-9997

Highview

Big Daddy’s BBQ

1205 N Garth Ave, Columbia, MO 65203

Being a family-owned business, Big Daddy’s BBQ has a wide variety of classic barbecue items. A restaurant favorite is the brisket sandwich and baby back ribs. They also have a ketchup-based house sauce that is a crowd favorite.

Website>> (573) 442-2884
